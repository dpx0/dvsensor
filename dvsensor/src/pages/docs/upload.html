Uploading a target sequence

Click on the "Generate sensors for an mRNA transcript" button on the start page. A page opens where you can upload your target mRNA sequence. You have two options for uploading a sequence: uploading a file containing a single-entry FASTA record or a GenBank record of your mRNA sequence, or by entering the FASTA/GenBank record manually (see below). The maximum size of a sequence is limited to 500KB.

To upload a file, click on the plus button in the right corner. Select a file and click the upload button (see below). To manually enter a sequence, paste the sequence into the text field and click "continue".
